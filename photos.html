Photos

拍照对我而言更像视觉版的笔记——记录我看世界的角度、光线、构图习惯。 以手机为主，偶有胶片。这里先保留三个长期主题：城市、人物剪影、日常物件。 我宁愿让页面保持克制，也不拿占位框假装已经完成。

/ 01

城市不是地标

费城、宁波、台北、亚特兰大。拍车站雨后的地面、旧城窄巷和人真正经过的地方，而不是明信片角度。

City · Light · Movement
/ 02

不打扰的肖像

人背对镜头、走进光里，或者只留下影子。比起辨认是谁，我更在意一个人如何占据空间。

People · Distance · Silhouette
/ 03

桌面考古

咖啡、鼓机、拆开的设备、写满批注的书。日常物件会泄露一个人真正把时间花在哪里。

Objects · Workbench · Routine

/ Photos

Photos

For me photography is more like visual note-taking — how I see the world, the light, the compositions I default to. Mostly phone, occasional film. This page holds three long-running themes: city, silhouettes, and everyday objects. I would rather keep it quiet than pretend placeholders are a finished archive.

/ 01

Cities are not landmarks

Philadelphia, Ningbo, Taipei, Atlanta. Wet pavement outside stations, narrow old-town alleys, and places people actually pass through — not postcard angles.

City · Light · Movement
/ 02

Portraits without interruption

People facing away, walking into light, or leaving only a shadow. I care less about who they are than how a person occupies space.

People · Distance · Silhouette
/ 03

Desktop archaeology

Coffee, drum machines, opened devices, heavily annotated books. Everyday objects reveal where someone actually spends their time.

Objects · Workbench · Routine
