Tianning Feng · 冯天宁

// Product · Research · Systems · Life

Born 2002 in Ningbo, China. BA at Emory in CS + Psychology, now MS at UPenn in ML × Systems. Academically I'm co-first author of SeizureFormer (PSB 2026 Oral) and a co-author on EpiDHGNN. In industry I led an LLM product feature at Liba Space, built a RAG-based lesson-planning platform for a gov-sponsored AI education project, and shipped a 100GB+ speech ETL pipeline for the Emory Language Biomarker Lab. Google PM certified and open to product roles — but more than that, I want this site to be the trace of how I think and build in the world. Drumming, photography, writing — they're all here.
