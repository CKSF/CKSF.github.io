Tianning Feng†, Juntong Ni†, Wei Jin, Ezequiel Gleichgerrcht

// Deep Learning · Time-series · Clinical AI

// Graph Learning · Epidemic Modeling · Hypergraph

提出 EpiDHGNN，一个面向传染病建模的动态超图神经网络。传统 GNN 只能处理成对交互， 但学校、职场、公共交通这类"多人同时接触"本质上是高阶关系。 我们用超图从"位置"和"个体"两个层级建模这种高阶交互，在源检测与传播预测任务上比 baseline 平均提升 ≈ 12.1%， 为公共卫生决策提供更可靠的模型输入。

SeizureFormer: A Multi-Scale Transformer for Seizure Risk Forecasting from RNS-Derived Biomarkers

Tianning Feng†, Juntong Ni†, Wei Jin, Ezequiel Gleichgerrcht
Pacific Symposium on Biocomputing 2026 · Oral (Top 10%) · † co-first author

// Deep Learning · Time-series · Clinical AI

A Transformer-based multi-scale model forecasting seizure risk 1–14 days ahead from structured IEA + LE biomarkers extracted by responsive neurostimulation (RNS) systems. Unlike raw scalp-EEG methods, SeizureFormer combines CNN patch embedding, cross-variable temporal convolution, and SE attention to jointly model short-term dynamics and long-term seizure cycles. Achieves SOTA across 5 patients × 4 windows — ROC AUC 79.44%, PR AUC 76.29%.

PSB 2026

Higher-order Interaction Matters: Modeling Epidemics via Dynamic Hypergraph Neural Networks

Songyuan Liu, Shengbo Gong, Tianning Feng, Zewen Liu, Max S. Y. Lau, Wei Jin
Pacific Symposium on Biocomputing 2026 · Open Access

// Graph Learning · Epidemic Modeling · Hypergraph

EpiDHGNN — a dynamic hypergraph neural network for epidemic modeling. Classical GNNs handle pairwise contact only, but real-world interactions in schools, offices, and transit are fundamentally higher-order. We use hypergraphs to model this from both location- and individual-level, beating baselines by ≈ 12.1% on source detection and forecasting tasks.
