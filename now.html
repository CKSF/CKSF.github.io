在想的问题

Agent 应该替人做多少决定？

Open

自动化越强，用户越容易失去判断过程。我在找一个边界：Agent 帮你减少机械操作，但关键决定仍然可见、可解释、可撤回。

工程师和产品经理，真的需要二选一吗？

Open

目前的答案是不需要。最适合我的位置，是能同时理解用户、定义问题，又能亲手把关键模块做出来的地方。

Open Questions

How much should an agent decide for its user?

Open

More automation can erase the user's reasoning process. I am looking for the boundary where agents remove mechanical work while consequential decisions stay visible, explainable, and reversible.

Do engineering and product really have to be separate identities?

Open

My current answer is no. The most natural place for me is one where I can understand users, define the problem, and still build the critical module myself.
